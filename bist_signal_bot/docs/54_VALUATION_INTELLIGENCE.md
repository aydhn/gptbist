# Valuation Intelligence (Phase 82)

The Valuation Intelligence layer provides deterministic, local-first research calculations for pricing multiples, historical bands, peer comparison, and risk scoring.

## Core Features
1. **Market Inputs**: Combines adjusted close prices, shares outstanding, and normalized financial statements.
2. **Multiples Calculation**: Deterministically calculates P/E, P/B, EV/EBITDA, EV/Sales, Price/Sales, FCF Yield, Earnings Yield, and Dividend Yield.
3. **Historical Bands**: Percentile rank and Z-score analysis of historical multiple bands.
4. **Peer Compare**: Discount/premium calculation vs sector medians.
5. **Risk Scoring**: Combines multiples, bands, data quality, and earnings quality to assign a Valuation Score (0-100) and Valuation Risk Level.
6. **Integrations**: Integrated directly into Scanners, Portfolio Construction, WhatIf, Explainability Cards, Review Inbox, and Daily Reports.

## Disclaimer
Outputs generated by this module are STRICTLY for local research and analysis. They do NOT constitute investment advice. Valuation bands and risk labels ("Cheap", "Expensive") do not guarantee price action. This module does NOT compute Target Prices or Fair Values. No real orders are sent.

## Example CLI Usage
`python -m bist_signal_bot valuation compute ASELS --save`
`python -m bist_signal_bot valuation report --symbol ASELS`
