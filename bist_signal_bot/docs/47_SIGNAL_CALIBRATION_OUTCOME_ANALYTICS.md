# Signal Calibration & Outcome Analytics (Phase 75)

## Overview
Phase 75 introduces the Signal Calibration and Outcome Analytics layer. It acts as an offline, research-only analytics engine to evaluate the real historical performance of signals generated by the bot. This allows the system to determine if high-confidence signals genuinely yield better outcomes and to tune signal thresholds properly.

## Architecture

1. **Outcome Dataset Builder**: Links raw signals to simulated trade outcomes (gross and net return percent), applying explicit `SUCCESS`, `FAILURE`, or `NEUTRAL` labels based on configurable thresholds.
2. **Calibration Bins**: Groups outcomes by score ranges (e.g., 0-10, 10-20... 90-100) to observe how the true success rate compares to the expected confidence in that band.
3. **Metrics & Reliability Curve**: Calculates core metrics like the Brier Score, Expected Calibration Error (ECE), Max Calibration Error (MCE), and Hit Rate. Generates Reliability Curves to visually and statistically measure confidence calibration.
4. **Signal Calibrator**: Takes the historical bins and generates a `CalibratorMapping`. This mapping converts raw scores into `calibrated_confidence` values closer to the observed probability of success.
5. **Threshold Tuner**: Optimizes cut-off thresholds for signal acceptance, observing the expected reduction in signal volume against the expected increase in net return quality. Produces `ThresholdPolicy` records as research recommendations.
6. **Error Taxonomy**: Categorizes errors (e.g., `HIGH_CONFIDENCE_FAILURE`, `EXECUTION_COST_DRAG`, `LIQUIDITY_FAILURE`) to detect specific systemic weaknesses in the strategy.
7. **Cohort Analysis**: Slices outcomes by strategy, symbol, sector, regime, and liquidity status to find weak cohorts that need adaptive adjustment.

## Gross vs Net Outcome
A primary feature is the distinction between Gross and Net outcomes. The engine evaluates expected slippage and cost drag. A signal with high gross return but severe cost drag might be re-classified as a failure (Execution Cost Drag).

## Integration
- **Scanner**: Calculates and appends `calibrated_confidence` to outputs when enabled.
- **Review & Evidence**: Surfaces `HIGH_CONFIDENCE_FAILURE` warnings in Evidence Cards and Analyst Inbox.
- **Strategy Registry**: Adjusts strategy scores based on the calibration error and cohort weakness count.

## Safety & Compliance
- **No Investment Advice**: The metrics and thresholds produced are purely mathematical outcomes of simulated testing.
- **Research-Only**: The `ThresholdPolicy` acts as a research-only indicator and does not authorize automated real trading.
- **No External Services**: No OpenAI, Web Scraping, or third-party APIs are used to produce these metrics.
